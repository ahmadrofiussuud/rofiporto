It began with a simple desire to automate my math homework. I stumbled upon Python, and soon, I was hooked. High school wasn't just about grades; it was my sandbox. I joined a robotics club, but my heart was always in the logic that powered the machines.

**Key Lessons:**
*   **Curiosity drives mastery:** I learned more from breaking things than from following tutorials.
*   **Logic is universal:** The problem-solving skills I picked up in math class translated directly to algorithms.
*   **Start small:** My first programs were simple scripts, but they taught me the joy of "Hello World."
